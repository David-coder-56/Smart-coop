About Us

Smart Coop was founded with a simple idea: to use smart technology to make poultry farming easier, smarter, and more sustainable. We understand the everyday challenges farmers face—long hours, rising costs, and the need to care for animals properly—so we built solutions that bring modern innovation directly to the coop.

From automated doors that protect flocks, to smart feeding and watering systems, to egg monitoring tools, Smart Coop is designed to save farmers time while boosting productivity and animal welfare.

Our team is passionate about combining agriculture and technology to create tools that are affordable, reliable, and farmer-friendly. Whether you're running a small backyard coop or managing a commercial farm, Smart Coop helps you take control, reduce stress, and focus on what matters most—healthy chickens and a successful farm.

Our Mission

At Smart Coop, we believe that technology should make farming efficient, sustainable, and profitable. Our mission is to empower chicken farmers with intelligent automation solutions that reduce labor, increase productivity, and improve animal welfare.

We are committed to developing cutting-edge IoT solutions that are accessible, reliable, and easy to use— whether you're a backyard enthusiast or managing a large commercial farm. By blending smart technology with practical farming needs, we aim to revolutionize poultry farming, making it stress-free, productive, and sustainable for everyone.

OUR TEAM

We are a team of passionate innovators, technologists, and agriculture enthusiasts working together to make farming smarter. Our mentors, partners, and local farmers guide us in shaping solutions that truly work.
